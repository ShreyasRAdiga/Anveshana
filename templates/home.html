Highlights:

In 2019, Our team won 1 Goldand 18 Silver medals and 4th Position among 98 participated colleges in State Level VTU Youth Festival held at BKIT, Bhalki which was held from 2nd to4th Nov 2018.

In 2018, Our team won 4 Gold,10 Silver and 10 Bronze medals and 4th Position among 92 participated colleges in State Level VTU Youth Festival held at Angadi Engineering College Premises, Belagavi, which was held from 11th to 13th April 2018.

In 2017, Our Team participated in the VTU Cultural Festival and won 1 Silver and 10 Bronze medal and placing JNNCE in the 6th Position among 95 participated colleges in VTU Youth Festival held at Sir.MVIT Premises, Bangalore, which was held from 16th to 19th March 2017.

In 2016, Our Team participated in the VTU Cultural Festival held at Alvas college of engineering, Mudabidri, from 7th to 10th May, 2016 and JNNCE Team EKYAM won the following Medals. 2 Silver Medals and 10 Bronze medal. Our college team was 8th position among 85 Colleges were participated.

Besides the VTU fest, our students have bagged many prizes in various inter collegiate cultural festivals organized by engineering and other colleges.
